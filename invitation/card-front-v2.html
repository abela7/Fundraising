The Liverpool Mekane Kidusan Abune Teklehymanot Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church (LMKAT EOTC) Diocese of Great Britain and Northern Ireland
Community engagement invitation
To

Praise be to the Almighty, we are pleased to announce that LMKAT EOTC will begin worshiping as St Gabriel's Church, located at 16 Yates Street, Liverpool, L8 6RD.

You are warmly invited to join us for a community engagement event, ahead of the consecration, to meet us, learn about the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church (EOTC), and explore opportunities for future collaboration.

Please join us to share your views and experience our ancient hymns, cultural cuisine, and traditional attire, as well as view the recent church renovations.

Date: Sunday, 29 March 2026
Time: 2pm to 5pm
