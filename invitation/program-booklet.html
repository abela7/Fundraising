14:00 – 14:05 Opening Prayer
In the name of the Father, and the Son, and the Holy Spirit, one God. Amen. Lord God, we thank You for bringing us together today in peace and unity. We thank You for this new church and this new beginning You have given us. As we gather, open our hearts and minds so that we may understand, learn, and grow in faith. Bless this time together and guide us in all that we do. Through the prayers of Saint Teklehaymanot and all Your saints, be with us now and always. Amen.
